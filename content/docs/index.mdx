## Welcome to WhatAisle

WhatAisle helps your shoppers find any product in your store. Your staff snap
casual photos of shelves, and your store grows a memory of what's where.
Shoppers scan a QR code and ask — by typing, speaking, or taking a photo — and
get a shelf number and a map.

## For store owners

### Getting started
1. **Sign up** and pick your store's permanent web address (like
   `yourstore.whataisle.com`). Choose carefully — it can't be changed.
2. **Send us a walk-through video** of your store. We draw your floor map by
   hand and send it back for you to confirm. You don't have to wait — you can
   start scanning shelves right away.
3. **Set a staff PIN** so your team can scan shelves without any accounts.
4. **Print the posters** (Posters & QR) and put them up so shoppers know they
   can scan to search.

### Keeping it accurate
- The more your staff photograph a product, the more trusted its location.
- Shoppers can tap "I looked — it's not there." After a few reports, that
  product is flagged for your staff to re-check. Feedback never changes your
  data on its own.
- Check **Insights** for what shoppers searched and what they couldn't find.

### Your data is yours
Export everything anytime, free, from **Export data**. Closing your store
deletes all of it permanently — so export a backup first.

## For staff

1. Open your store's page and go to the staff area.
2. Enter the store PIN.
3. Pick the shelf you're standing in front of.
4. Take photos or choose them from your phone. The system recognizes the
   products automatically — no typing.
5. Remove anything that looks wrong, then tap **Save**.

That's it. The more you snap, the better shoppers can find things.
